Getting Started

Splits is a simple and intuitive race timer. Tap Start to begin timing, Lap to record a split, and Stop to finish. Your timings are saved automatically and synced across your devices via iCloud.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Make sure you are signed into the same iCloud account on all your devices. Your timing data will sync automatically.

How do I organise my timings?

Create categories (e.g., "50m Freestyle", "400m") using the + button on the home screen. Each timing session is saved under the selected category.

Can I add notes to a timing?

Yes. After stopping a timer, you can add a comment to record details about the session.

Why is location being requested?

Splits can optionally capture your location when you start a timing session. This helps you remember where you were training. You can deny location access and the app will work normally without it.

How do I delete a timing?

Swipe left on any timing in the history list to delete it.
